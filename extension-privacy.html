Privacy Policy

“محاضر amatti ⇋ ListMaster” extension — Last updated: 2026

This extension is a fully local tool that helps school staff download their own official reports from the Algerian Ministry of Education portal amatti.education.dz as Excel files.

Data the extension accesses

When you click a download button, the extension reads class-council reports (student IDs, names, averages, decisions) from amatti.education.dz within your own logged-in session — data you are already authorized to access as the institution's staff member.

How it is processed

This data is processed entirely locally inside your browser: it is converted into an Excel file saved to your own device.

What the extension does NOT do

  • ❌ It does not send any data to the developer or to any third-party server.
  • ❌ It does not communicate with any domain other than amatti.education.dz.
  • ❌ It contains no tracking, analytics, or advertising.
  • ❌ It stores nothing on any remote server; the developer collects no information about you.

Permissions & why

  • host_permissions: amatti.education.dz — to read your reports from the portal within your session.
  • scripting — to run the gathering logic inside the portal page (same-origin, your own cookies).
  • downloads — to save the resulting Excel file to your device.
